About us

Who are we?

We are a group of students of the faculty of technology, arts and design from Oslo Metropolitan University. Our group was assigned to complete a final project in the course “Web development and inclusive design” (in Norwegian “Webutvikling og inkluderende design”). The aim of the project was to come up with a solution to one of UN’s development goals and to make a webpage to present our solution.

You can read more about the assignment here and our proposed solution here.
